POLY AGENT GIFT - START HERE
============================

1) Double-click:
   OPEN_SETUP_GUIDE.cmd

2) In the .env file that opens, fill your account values.
   For Magic/email users:
   - POLY_SIGNATURE_TYPE=1
   - POLY_FUNDER_ADDRESS = wallet shown on polymarket settings/profile

3) Double-click:
   SETUP_FIRST_TIME.cmd

4) Validate auth:
   VERIFY_ENV_LINK.cmd

5) Start:
   START_AGENT.cmd
   START_DASHBOARD.cmd

6) Open:
   http://localhost:7731

PowerShell note
---------------
If you already have PowerShell open in this folder, the `.ps1` versions work too.
For most guests, the `.cmd` launchers are the easiest and most reliable option.
